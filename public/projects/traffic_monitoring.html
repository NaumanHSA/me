🚦 Real-Time Road Traffic Monitoring System using YOLOv5


Figure: Live detection, classification, and tracking of vehicles with brand and color recognition.

🌟 Project Overview

The Real-Time Road Traffic Monitoring System is an advanced computer vision platform designed to analyze live road traffic footage with remarkable precision and speed. It integrates deep learning models to detect, classify, and track vehicles while extracting rich details like vehicle brand and color in real time.

This project showcases how modern machine learning can be applied to smart transportation systems, enabling data-driven insights for urban planning, traffic management, and public safety.

🚀 Key Features

🛻 Vehicle Detection & Classification

  • Built on a custom-trained YOLOv5 small model tailored for real-time performance.
  • Accurately detects and classifies vehicles into five categories:
    • Car
    • Truck
    • Bus
    • Motorcycle
    • Bicycle
  • Handles both dense urban traffic and sparse highway scenes.

🎨 Vehicle Color Recognition

  • Implements a lightweight Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) model developed in Keras.
  • Trained to identify seven vehicle colors:
    • White, Black, Red, Blue, Orange, Green, Yellow.
  • Uses a custom-curated dataset with thousands of vehicle images labeled by color.
  • Achieves fast inference while maintaining reliable accuracy, even in varied lighting conditions.

🚗 Vehicle Brand Recognition

  • Leverages a second YOLOv5 model specialized in detecting vehicle logos from cropped images.
  • Recognizes 13 popular automotive brands, including:
    • Audi, Mercedes, BMW, Honda, Toyota, Hyundai, Opel, Peugeot, Renault, Skoda, Volkswagen, FIAT, Ford.
  • Enables advanced analytics such as brand-specific traffic flow statistics or market analysis.

🎥 Real-Time Object Tracking & Vehicle Counting

  • Integrates the Deep SORT tracking algorithm:
    • Assigns unique IDs to each vehicle.
    • Maintains vehicle identities across frames for accurate counting and movement analysis.
  • Ensures precise vehicle counts and helps monitor traffic density over time.

💡 Region of Interest (ROI) Filtering

  • Allows the definition of specific regions within the video frame for focused analysis.
  • Helps avoid false detections from irrelevant areas like sidewalks, pedestrians, or roadside objects.
  • Supports flexible configurations to tailor the system for different camera setups and monitoring goals.

📊 Data Logging & Analytics

  • For every vehicle detected, the system captures:
    • Vehicle type
    • Color
    • Brand
    • Tracking ID
    • Location details
  • Compiles the data into structured logs for:
    • Traffic flow analysis
    • Statistical reporting
    • Historical data mining

🖼️ Rich Visualization

  • Provides high-quality visual overlays showing:
    • Bounding boxes around vehicles
    • Labels for type, color, and brand
    • Tracking IDs for each object
  • Delivers intuitive insights to traffic operators or analysts reviewing recorded footage.

🎯 Practical Applications

The system’s capabilities make it highly valuable for:

  • Smart City Infrastructure
    • Traffic optimization
    • Incident detection
  • Urban Planning
    • Vehicle flow statistics
    • Road usage studies
  • Law Enforcement
    • Identifying specific vehicles of interest
    • Vehicle searches by brand or color
  • Market Analytics
    • Automotive brand distribution studies
    • Consumer behavior insights

💻 Technical Highlights

  • Combines multiple specialized deep learning models into a cohesive pipeline.
  • Balances accuracy and real-time speed, critical for live deployments.
  • Custom dataset development for:

  • Robust performance in challenging conditions:
